## Primary References

### Centore (2020) - Primary Methodology

**Citation:**
Centore, P. (2020). Beige, aqua, fuchsia, etc.: Definitions for some non-basic surface colour names. *Journal of the International Colour Association*, 25, 24-54.

**Download URL:**
https://www.munsellcolourscienceforpainters.com/ColourSciencePapers/BeigeAquaFuchsiaEtc.pdf

**Local file:** `jaic_v25_03.pdf`
**Downloaded:** 2024-08-09

**Summary:**
Provides definitions for 30 non-basic English colour names using convex polyhedra in Munsell space. Includes methodology for constructing inner convex hulls to handle outliers. This paper forms the primary basis for the colour name classification system.

**Key contributions:**
- Inner convex hull methodology for outlier-robust color region definition
- 30 polyhedra definitions in Munsell space
- CAUS (Color Association of the United States) data source

### Lay (2007) - Mathematical Foundations

**Citation:**
Lay, S. R. (2007). *Convex Sets and Their Applications*. Dover Publications.

**ISBN:** 978-0486458038

**Publisher:** Dover Publications (reprint of 1982 Wiley edition)

**Local file:** `convex-sets-and-their-applications.pdf`

**Note:** This is a book - no direct download URL available. Available for purchase from Amazon, Dover, or academic libraries.

**Summary:**
Provides mathematical foundations for convex set theory, including minimal generating sets and Krein-Milman theorem. Underpins the polyhedra-based approach to colour name definitions.
